# RAKSHAK AI

## Predict. Prevent. Protect.

RAKSHAK AI is a computer system that uses artificial intelligence to keep people safe from bad guys on the internet. It helps stop police scams, cyber fraud and fake money from being used. The system uses lots of technologies like artificial intelligence, graph analytics, computer vision and geospatial intelligence to help people, banks, phone companies and police work together to find and stop threats before anyone loses money.

## Problem Statement

In India there are a lot of things happening on the internet like fake police scams, financial fraud and fake money. The current systems are not very good at stopping these things because they only work after someone has already been hurt and lost money.

We need a system that can look at all the information and use intelligence to find and stop bad guys before they can hurt anyone.

## Our Solution

RAKSHAK AI is a system that brings all the information together and uses intelligence to find suspicious activities uncover hidden bad guy networks predict when bad things might happen and help keep people safe.

The system is designed to help police and other groups move from reacting to bad things after they happen to actually preventing them from happening in the first place.

## Key Features

### Digital Arrest Scam Detection

* The system can identify scam scripts

* It can detect when someone is pretending to be a police officer or other authority figure

* It analyzes how scammers try to scare people

* It gives a score to show how risky something is and sends out alerts

### Fraud Network Intelligence

* The system looks at how phone numbersre connected

* It maps out bank accounts and UPI relationships

* It groups devices. Accounts together

* It identifies groups of guys working together

### Counterfeit Currency Detection

* The system checks the security thread on money

* It validates the watermark

* It analyzes the microprint

* It verifies the number

### GeoShield Crime Intelligence

* The system maps out areas where bad things happen a lot

* It identifies areas that're at high risk

* It visualizes crime trends

* It monitors threats and predicts when bad things might happen

### Citizen Fraud Shield

* The system provides help to people through WhatsApp

* It assesses the risk of messages

* It helps people report fraud

* It educates people on how to stay
